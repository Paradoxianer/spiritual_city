# ⏫ Progression & Stage System

In SpiritWorld City, you grow stronger just by playing. Every use of your resources contributes to your experience.

## Resource Levels

Each of your four main resources (Faith, Materials, Health, Hunger) has its own level.
*   **Leveling Up**: Spending or gathering Faith increases your Faith level. The same applies to all other resources.
*   **Reward**: With each level, the **maximum limit** of that resource increases permanently (e.g., from 100 to 120 Max Faith).

## Visual HUD Indicator

In the top-left HUD, you see a thin, glowing **pixel strip** under each resource bar. This shows you how close you are to the next level.

## Why is it Important?

A higher maximum is crucial for prayer combat. The more Faith you can hold, the more powerful your prayer strikes become in the spiritual world.

## Permanent Modifiers

Besides resource levels, you unlock permanent benefits through certain milestones:
*   **Strength**: Increases your prayer power (after 3 conversions).
*   **Fervor**: Widens the optimal timing window for prayer (after 10 combats).
*   **Wisdom**: Lowers the Faith cost per prayer (after 20 conversations).
