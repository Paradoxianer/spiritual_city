Welcome to **SpiritWorld City**. You take on the role of a **pastor** whose goal is to transform a city filled with darkness.

## Game Goal
Your mission is twofold:
1.  **Liberation**: Transform the dark (red) areas of the spiritual world into light (green) zones.
2.  **Conversion**: Gain the city's inhabitants for the faith. The more light there is in an area, the more open people are to your message.

## The Core Gameplay Loop

1.  **Start at the Pastor's House**: This is your safe haven. By praying in the house, you already influence the spiritual world positively and create an initial radius of action.
2.  **The Spiritual World (Key Q)**: Switch to the invisible world to see the city's state. Use prayer combat to cleanse the first area. If too many daemons appear, retreat to the real world.
3.  **Serving Neighbors**: Talk to people in the city. If they are in a "liberated" (green) zone, counseling and reading the Bible together are easier.
4.  **Fellowship**: When you pray with already converted Christians, it has a particularly strong effect on the spiritual world and helps you create connected zones of light.
