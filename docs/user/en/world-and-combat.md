# 🌌 The Spiritual World & Prayer Combat

The **Spiritual World** is a reflection of the city showing its spiritual state. Your goal as a pastor is to spread the light (Green) and push back the darkness (Red).

## Territory State

Every meter of the city has a value from -100 to +100:

*   🟢 **Deep Green (+80 to +100)**: The area is liberated. People here are more open to faith.
*   ⚪ **White/Beige (-30 to +30)**: Neutral ground. This is where the battle happens.
*   🔴 **Dark Red (-60 to -100)**: Stronghold of darkness. Daemons can spawn here.

## Daemons

In dark areas, you will encounter daemons. They circle you and try to weaken you.
*   **Contact**: If a daemon touches you, you lose Health, Hunger, and Faith.
*   **Slime Trail**: Daemons darken the ground they move over.

## Combat Tactics

Use the different prayer modes (keys 1-4) to control daemons:
*   Use **Slow (3)** if you are surrounded by many daemons.
*   Use **Rebuke (2)** to keep daemons at a distance.
*   Use **Liberation (1)** to permanently cleanse areas.
*   Use **Drain (4)** when your Faith is running low.

**Important**: Prayers have an Area of Effect (AoE). The longer you hold Space and the better your timing, the larger the impact on the environment.
